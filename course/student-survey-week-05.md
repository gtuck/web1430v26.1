# WEB 1430 — Mid-Course Check-In Survey (Week 5)

**Purpose:** This survey helps improve the course for you and future students. Your responses are anonymous. Please answer honestly — there are no right or wrong answers.

**Takes approximately:** 5–8 minutes

**How to complete it:** Use the anonymous survey form or Canvas survey link provided by your instructor. This page is the canonical prompt set for that survey.
