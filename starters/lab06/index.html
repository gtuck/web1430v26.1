Campus Services

Frequently Asked Questions

Log in to the student portal at my.weber.edu and navigate to the Registration tab. Search for courses by subject, instructor, or CRN number.

The financial aid office is located in the Shepherd Union building, room 175. Hours are Monday–Friday, 8am–5pm.

Parking permits are purchased online through the campus parking portal. Annual permits go on sale in July; semester permits are available year-round.

Free tutoring is available through the Student Success Center in the Stewart Library. Book appointments online or walk in during drop-in hours.

Connect to the "WildcatWireless" network and sign in with your W number and password. Visit IT Support in the library if you have trouble connecting.

Printing is available in the library, the Shepherd Union computer labs, and most academic buildings. Each student receives a $10 print credit per semester.

Campus Resources

Stewart Library

Hours: Monday–Thursday 7am–10pm · Friday 7am–6pm · Saturday 9am–6pm · Sunday 1pm–9pm

Services include printing, scanning, study rooms (book online), research consultations, and a 24-hour computer lab.

Student Recreation Center

Hours: Monday–Friday 6am–11pm · Saturday–Sunday 8am–8pm

Includes a fitness floor, indoor track, pool, climbing wall, and group fitness classes. Free with a valid student ID.

Campus Dining

The Marketplace (Shepherd Union): Monday–Friday 7am–8pm · Weekends 10am–6pm

The Grill (Student Services Building): Monday–Friday 8am–3pm

Meal plans are available through Student Services. Dining dollars can be added to your Wildcat Card at any time.
