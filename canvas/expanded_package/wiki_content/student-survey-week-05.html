WEB 1430 — Mid-Course Check-In Survey (Week 5)

Purpose: This survey helps improve the course for you and future students. Your responses are anonymous. Please answer honestly — there are no right or wrong answers.

Takes approximately: 5–8 minutes

How to complete it: Use the anonymous survey form or Canvas survey link provided by your instructor. This page is the canonical prompt set for that survey.

Section 1: Pacing and Cognitive Load

1. Overall, how would you describe the pace of the course so far (Weeks 1–5)?

  • [ ] Too slow — I often feel like I'm waiting for harder material
  • [ ] About right — I feel challenged but not overwhelmed
  • [ ] Slightly fast — I occasionally struggle to keep up
  • [ ] Too fast — I regularly feel lost or behind

2. Which week or topic felt most overwhelming? (Select all that apply)

  • [ ] Week 1 — Browser fundamentals and DevTools
  • [ ] Week 2 — HTML semantics and mobile-first CSS

  • [ ] Week 4 — Control flow, functions, and scope
  • [ ] Week 5 — Arrays, objects, and JSON
  • [ ] None — I haven't felt overwhelmed
  • [ ] Other: ___________

3. On average, how many hours per week are you spending on this course (lectures, labs, assignments, and projects combined)?

  • [ ] Less than 5 hours
  • [ ] 5–8 hours
  • [ ] 9–12 hours
  • [ ] 13–16 hours
  • [ ] More than 16 hours

Section 2: Content and Clarity

4. How clearly do the textbook chapters explain the required concepts?
